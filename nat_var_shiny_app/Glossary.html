Glossary

Nucleotide Diversity

A statistic used to describe the diversity of a genetic sequence in a given population or sample. Nucleotide diversity for a given sequence can be defined as the probability of drawing two sequences at random from the popuation that are not identical

Single Nucleotide Polymorphism (SNP)

A single nucelotide polymorhpism is a difference of a single nucleotide in genomes of idividuals of a population. This can include a change in base, or a insertion or deletion. For the data analysed here, insertions of more than one nucleotide are included, but insertions and deletions are generally uncommon on protein coding regions of active genes.
